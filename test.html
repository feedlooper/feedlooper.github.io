The Loop

A practical bridge between waste, feed, and soil.

FeedLoop is designed as a circular operating system: organic material enters, biology converts it, and products return to agriculture.

01 / INPUT

Organic by-products

Pre-consumer food and agricultural streams from processors, farms, packers, and distributors.

02 / CONVERSION

Larvae bioconversion

Black Soldier Fly larvae convert organic material into nutrient-rich biomass.

03 / PROCESSING

Separation and finishing

Larvae biomass and residual frass are processed into usable product streams.

04 / MARKET

Feed and agriculture

Protein, oil, and fertilizer outputs move back into regional supply chains.

About

Not waste management. Circular production infrastructure.

FeedLoop exists to help food and agriculture systems capture more value from organic by-products. We are building a platform that makes circularity practical, local, and commercially useful.

Positioning

We turn disposal problems into production pathways.

Organic streams should not only be diverted. They should become feedstock for the next layer of food and agriculture.

Regional focus

Western Canada first.

Built around local supply chains, local processors, and local agricultural markets.

Model

Input. Convert. Return.

A simple operating model that connects waste suppliers, feed buyers, and fertilizer buyers.

Approach

Biology with infrastructure discipline.

Natural conversion, designed for repeatable and scalable deployment.

Technology

High-efficiency biology inside a controlled system.

Black Soldier Fly larvae are the biological engine. FeedLoop’s role is to build the intake, production, processing, and market pathways around them.

Biology

Black Soldier Fly larvae

A natural converter of organic material into nutrient-dense biomass.

Operations

Controlled production

Consistent intake, preparation, rearing, drying, and product separation.

Infrastructure

Designed to scale with regional demand.

Modular deployment means the system can grow with available feedstock and product markets.

Products

Three outputs from one circular process.

The value of the system is in what it produces: ingredients and inputs that can move back into feed and agriculture.

01

Insect protein meal

A sustainable protein ingredient for animal feed applications, including poultry, aquaculture, pet food, and feed formulation markets.

02

Insect oil

A functional fat source that creates additional value from larvae biomass and can support feed and pet nutrition applications.

03

Frass fertilizer

An organic soil input that returns nutrients and biological value to growers, helping close nutrient loops.
