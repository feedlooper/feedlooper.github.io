Circular food infrastructure

Food waste should not be wasted.

We turn organic by-products into protein, oil, and fertilizer through Black Soldier Fly bioconversion.

The loop

From organic by-products to agricultural inputs.

FeedLoop connects waste suppliers, feed markets, and growers through one local circular system.

01 / INPUT

Organic by-products

Pre-consumer food and agriculture streams from processors, farms, packers, and distributors.

02 / CONVERSION

BSF larvae

Black Soldier Fly larvae convert organic material into nutrient-dense biomass.

03 / OUTPUTS

Protein, oil, frass

The process creates feed ingredients and organic fertilizer products.

04 / RETURN

Back to agriculture

Outputs return to feed and soil markets, keeping value circulating locally.

About

Building a local circular protein system.

FeedLoop is developing a regional bioconversion platform for Western Canada. Our goal is to turn underused organic streams into practical products for feed, fertilizer, and agriculture.

01

Waste becomes input.

Organic by-products are treated as the beginning of a new supply chain, not the end of one.

02

Biology does the work.

BSF larvae create a high-efficiency conversion pathway from organics to usable biomass.

03

Value stays local.

Outputs support nearby feed, fertilizer, and agriculture markets.

Technology

Bioconversion infrastructure for organic streams.
